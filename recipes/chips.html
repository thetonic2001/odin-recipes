Baked Tortilla Chips

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Lime Juice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cut the tortillas into triangles â€” you should be able to get about eight chips out of each tortilla.
  2. Season the Chips Add lime juice and oil to a spray bottle and spray each chip. You can also brush the oil and lime juice mixture with a pastry brush. Sprinkle each chip with the spices.
  3. Bake the chips, flipping and re-seasoning halfway through. Make sure to check them often so they don't burn.
